Reviews

... it was a great weekend, packed with wonderful dance instructions and opportunities to apply what we learned. The mix of instructors and styles was inspiring, I really enjoyed your ideas of incorporating elements from WCS into cross step waltz. The instructors really worked well together and it set the tone for the whole group to enjoy each other in learning and exploring and sharing. Through the lessons and talks I came to develop a much better understanding of cross step waltz, the ease of its basic steps and opportunities for variations and how it compares to other social dances. I also really liked the lunch with several pre‑ordered choices, a few extras and special care for the recycling of the packaging and leftovers. Michael's instructions on the cleanup were exceptionally clear and helpful. His whole space embodied care and spiritual awareness, a welcome reprieve from the common industrial and run-of-the mill look and decor. — DM
I wanted to thank you for organizing a truly wonderful workshop weekend. Even if I didn’t always show it (severe back pain), I thoroughly enjoyed this workshop. The collaborative theme was perfect and all of the instructors emulated this theme throughout the weekend. Further more all of the instructors seemed to have the same philosophy towards teaching cross step and the lessons dovetailed on each other seamlessly. The pacing of instruction and the workshop itself was just right. While the cookies were delicious, the real dessert after lunch were the instructor talks. I left on Sunday feeling full, yet curious and wanting to learn more, empowered to explore more of the creative side (hard to imagine a Westie wanting to be creative, I know), and appreciative of the depth of knowledge and quality of instruction for the weekend. — MO
